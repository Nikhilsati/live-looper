Keep these points in mind while designing the app:
- Think like an artist who is performing live.
- Think in terms of planning, reherarsing and performing.
- Ease of use is very important. Less mental load on the user. He should focus on his performance and not on the app.
- Make the UI touch friendly. Large buttons, easy to reach controls. Minimalist UI, especially in Live mode.
- The app should feel very fast and responsive.
